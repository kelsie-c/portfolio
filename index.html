    About Me

    I'm a Junior Web Developer in Columbus, Ohio, strengthening my skills by attening the Ohio State University's Coding Bootcamp to become a Full Stack Developer. I have a B.A. in Communications and a Minor in Design and am incredibly excited to get started in this new industry!

    I'm also on the board of a local non-profit, Student Success Stores, where I help support the development and operations of the organization. In the age of COVID-19, our students are facing extraordinary circumstances, and Student Success Stores aims to bridge the gap to provide them with access to basic essentials. I am truly honored to be a part of this mission.
